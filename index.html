About Me

Full Stack Web Developer

Ever since I was a kid, I dreamed of becoming a computer engineer. I still remember writing it in my school slam book—I didn't fully understand what it meant back then, but I knew I wanted to build things with computers.


That childhood dream stayed with me, even through all the ups and downs. The journey wasn't easy—there were times I felt lost, frustrated, and even thought about quitting. But I kept pushing myself, learning through every setback, and eventually found my path.


Today, I'm a passionate Fullstack Developer with 5 years of experience building modern, responsive web applications. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Node.js, crafting clean, efficient code that brings designs to life and delivers powerful user experiences.
